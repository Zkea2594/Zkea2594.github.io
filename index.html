My Projects

Blockmesh Level Design

Here is my latest blockmesh design. I wanted to protray more of a greek/mediterranian theme for the setting. I also wanted to convey more movement to add a sense of direction. Utilizing carts and lights to simulate breadcrumbs for the player to follow. Providing an aqueduct to give the player a direction to look where water is flowing from. Add in birds circling overhead to signify a sense of importance. Further in the player comes across a gated path. I utilized trees to lead the way and climbable surfaces to give the player sense of resistance. Later on the player is forced to cross a gap without a safe way to return. This creates a valve barring the player until a later condition is met. Upon reaching the temple, a gorgeous view of the city is available, and the door is interactable. With the door I wanted to symbolize the heaviness of the doors and made them move slowly.
